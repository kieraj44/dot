For Your Tax Refund
  • How can i direct deposit my tax refund to my Green Dot Account? faq:AddingMoney/DirectDeposit/DirectDepositsForTaxRefund

    All you have to do to receive your tax refund on your Green Dot card is:

    1. Get your routing and direct deposit account number by logging in – or – through text.
    2. Use this information when asked for your routing and direct deposit account number.
    3. When asked for Green Dot’s bank routing number, use the following: 124303120
    4. For tax refund purposes, be sure to mark the box labeled “checking” for the type of account.
    5. Sit back, relax, and enjoy getting your tax refund faster than a paper check!


  • How do I check the status of my tax refund? faq:AddingMoney/DirectDeposit/DirectDepositsForTaxRefund

    We do not have the status of your tax refund. However, you can get an update on the status of your refund through the IRS by:

    • Visiting www.irs.gov/refunds and going to “Where’s My Refund.”
    • Downloading the IRS2Go App for your mobile device.
    • Calling the IRS Refund Hotline at (800) 829-1954.

    For more info on tax refunds, visit www.irs.gov/refunds.

  • Why should I have my tax refund deposited to a Green Dot account? faq:AddingMoney/DirectDeposit/DirectDepositsForTaxRefund

    Direct deposit is a FREE and easy way to receive your tax refund faster than a paper check! Avoid waiting by the mailbox, making extra trips, and paying extra fees just so you can cash or deposit your tax refund. Best of all, receiving your tax refund through direct deposit means the money is automatically loaded to your card so that you can access and use the funds from your refund.

    3. Log in to your account online.

    4. Call (866) 795-7974 and enter your card information.

  • Can I receive tax refunds for other people in my account? faq:AddingMoney/DirectDeposit/DirectDepositsForTaxRefund

    No, you cannot direct other’s refunds to your account or card.

If we receive a tax refund intended for individuals other than the account or cardholder, the refund will be rejected and/or your card/account may be blocked, pending additional verification. Get more details from the IRS about tax refunds here.

For Your Payroll or Government Benefits
  • Do I have to direct deposit my entire paycheck? How much of my pay can I have direct deposited? faq:AddingMoney/DirectDeposit/DirectDepositForPayrollOrGovBenefits

    With the exception of certain federal government benefit payments, or your employer or payroll department's restrictions, you can choose to have as much or as little of your paycheck or benefits payment deposited to your Green Dot card as you’d like. The choice is completely yours, and you can cancel or change the amount of your direct deposit at any time.

    If you enroll in direct deposit for Social Security, Supplemental Security Income, Veterans Benefits, or Federal Civil Service, the entire amount of your benefit payment must be deposited to your Green Dot card. You can cancel the direct deposit of your federal government benefits at any time, but you cannot change the amount of your federal benefits direct deposit.

    Some employers and benefits providers send payment instructions ahead of the actual payday. If they do, we’ll credit the amount to your card when we receive payment instructions (instead of waiting for the actual payday) so you can enjoy your money sooner. However, it’s important to note that if and how early your deposit can be posted to your account is completely dependent on when the employer or benefits provider sends us the payment instructions and fraud restrictions may apply. As such, the availability or timing of early direct deposit may vary from pay period to pay period. Make sure the name and social security number on file with your employer or benefits provider matches what’s on your account exactly. We will not be able to deposit your payment if we are unable to match recipients.

Bank Transfer

  • My bank transfer was rejected. Why? faq:AddingMoney/BankTransfer

    There are a few reasons why your bank transfer can be rejected:

    • The bank account you’re transferring from may not have enough funds in it to make the transfer.

    • The bank account you’re transferring from may be closed.

    • The login credentials for the bank account you’re transferring from have been updated.  In the event this happens, you will be asked to re-link your bank account with the updated login credentials. 

  • Can I transfer money from my Green Dot account to another bank account? faq:AddingMoney/BankTransfer

    No, you cannot transfer money from your Green Dot account to another bank account. At this time, you can only receive money to your Green Dot account from another bank.

  • Can I edit or cancel a scheduled one-time or recurring bank transfer? faq:AddingMoney/BankTransfer

    Yes, you can edit or cancel a scheduled one-time or recurring bank transfer by logging in to the Green Dot mobile app and tapping the transaction under Scheduled transfers. From there you can edit or delete the transfer. Edits or cancellations must be made before 11:50pm PST on the day before the next scheduled transfer date.

  • How do I link another bank account to my Green Dot account? faq:AddingMoney/BankTransfer

    To link to another bank account, you must:

    1. Log in to your Green Dot account.

    2. On the website select “Add Funds” or in the app tap “Deposit”.

    3. Select Bank Transfer from the menu.

    4. Select the bank you want to link.
