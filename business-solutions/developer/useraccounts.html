Users & Accounts

This API allows the updating and retrieval of current user and account data, such as personal information, transaction history, account details, account balances, available statements, and identity verification. 

 

How is this helpful?

The ability to update and retrieve a user's information along with their account data is vital to providing great customer service. It is also imperative for product expansion, because it provides valuable insight into the target audience.
